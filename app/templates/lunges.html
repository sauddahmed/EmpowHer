How To Do A Lunge
  • Stand tall with feet hip-width apart and engage core muscles.
  • Step forward with one leg, keeping your straight and aligned.
  • Bend both knees to about degrees, lowering your hips.
  • Push off your front leg to return to the starting position.
  • Similiarly repeat the movement with your opposite leg for same number of reps.
