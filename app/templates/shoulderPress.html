How To Do A Shoulder Press
  • Stand tall with feet shoulder-width apart and engage.
  • Hold a dumbbell in each hand at height with facing forward.
  • Press the weights upward until arms fully extended overhead.
  • Lower the dumbbells back down to shoulder height with.
  • Repeat the movement for several repetitions, focusing on stability.
