Why Pushups Are Important ?

Pushups are a versatile and effective exercise for building upper body strength and improving overall fitness. Here are some key benefits:

  • Enhances muscle strength and endurance in the chest, shoulders, and triceps.
  • Engages the core muscles, helping to improve stability and balance.
  • Helps improve posture by strengthening the muscles that support the spine.
  • Increases overall functional fitness, making everyday tasks easier.
  • Boosts cardiovascular health when done in high repetitions or as part of a circuit.
