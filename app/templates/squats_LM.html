Why Squats Are Important ?

Squats are a fundamental exercise for building lower body strength and improving overall fitness. Here are some key benefits:

  • Strengthens the muscles in your legs, including the quadriceps, hamstrings, and calves.
  • Engages the core muscles, improving stability and balance.
  • Helps improve posture by strengthening the muscles of the lower back and abdomen.
  • Can enhance athletic performance by increasing power and speed.
  • Supports joint health by improving flexibility and range of motion in the hips and knees.
  • Boosts metabolic rate and helps with weight management.
